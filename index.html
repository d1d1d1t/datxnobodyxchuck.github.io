Bio


ORIGINS

DNC was born (June 16 1991) and developed in the streets of Las Vegas NV. At 26 years old, DnC is a proud father and husband; two of his proudest accomplishments. DnC, also known as Charles E. Ford Jr is also a mentor with Apex fun run, an opportunity he says: "Gives me a chance to be a good influence on kids, aside from all the negative ones out there." He is also a barber by trade and donates free haircuts to children at the Boys and Girls Club every quarter.

PROJECTS:

Currently DnC is working on a follow up to his debut mixtape, Sin 616 (see I'm nobody 616).

We The People:
On his official debut album, We The People, DnC takes on all aspects of today's struggle: failure and success, the definition of what's real versus what's fake, love, loyalty, as well as other aspects of life.

"I wanted to make an album that didn't reflect DnC, but reflected what the majority of us experience while we alive." -- DnC

DNC has performed on Coast 2 Coast and has aslo opened up for the Dove Shack and DJ Screw on the Grind It Out Tour.

DnCs MUSIC

DnC's delivery is a combination of conscious, thought-driven bars, combined with melodic backgrounds, and punchy beats. In his own words, his style is categorized as "Revolutionary Flow, where hip-hop meets neo-soul."

WHAT DRIVES DnC:

His main influence is God followed closely by his wife and kids. He admires artists like Tupac ,jcole and Kendrick Lamar. However, he began persuing a career as a rapper, citing Lil Wayne as "the reason for that." DnC is also a fan on Flavor Flav and Dougie Fresh.
